    Humidity

    There is no strong correlation between latitude and humidity.


    Traditionally, the northern hemisphere is known to be more humid than the southern hemisphere which has larger ocean area. This is supported by the data which shows a concentration of cities in northern hemisphere with humidity between 60-80%. However, it is interesting to note: (1) there is a strong band of cities throughout both hemispheres at 100% humidity, (2) the northern hemisphere has a series of outliers at 0%.
